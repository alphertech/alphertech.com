Retail POS Modernization — GreenMarket

Modernizing checkout and inventory visibility across multiple stores with a POS that works online and offline.

Project Snapshot

  • Client: GreenMarket (regional retail chain)
  • Focus: faster checkout, reliable sync, inventory reconciliation, management reporting
  • Engagement: POS modernization + rollout support across stores
  • Deliverables: register app (offline-first), sync layer, inventory workflows, KPI dashboard

What we were fixing

GreenMarket operated across 18 locations on a legacy, on-premise POS setup. During busy periods, stores saw long checkout times and frequent sync delays—making inventory visibility and reporting unreliable for managers.

Key challenges

  • Inventory mismatch: counts drifted due to offline activity and delayed updates.
  • Checkout friction: slow, inconsistent payment and item-scanning workflows during peaks.
  • Reporting lag: manual processes delayed daily decisions on stock and promotions.

What we changed

We replaced the legacy workflow with a cloud-backed POS that continues working even when connectivity is unstable:

  • Offline-capable register: reliable local operation with queue-based sync when network returns.
  • Inventory reconciliation: controlled update rules to reduce conflicts across stores.
  • Faster checkout flow: streamlined item lookup and hardware integration for scanners and receipt printers.
  • Manager dashboard: daily KPIs, sales segmentation, and actionable alerts.

How we built it (technology)

We implemented the register UI and sync workflows using React for the interface, Node.js services for business logic and synchronization, and PostgreSQL for transactional data. Hosting and background processing were managed on AWS using standard storage and compute services.

Results

  • Checkout speed: average checkout time improved by ~40% during peak hours.
  • Inventory accuracy: reconciliation improved from ~85% to ~98% over the first two months after rollout.
  • Operational visibility: managers received updates more quickly for restocking and promotion decisions.
  • Business impact: GreenMarket reported an uplift in weekly sales associated with checkout reliability.

Client quote

"AlpherTech's POS changes made our busiest days smoother—customers notice it, and our managers can trust the numbers." — Operations Director, GreenMarket
