What is the Seytrons network?

The Seytrons network is a private, secure connection that routes your traffic through an encrypted tunnel. The Seytrons app keeps your browsing private, blocks intrusive ads, and helps you stay fast on any WiFi.

Is Seytrons free WiFi in Kampala?

Seytrons is a free WiFi and privacy app made in Kampala, Uganda. It helps you get a safer, smoother connection on public and home WiFi across Kampala and beyond.

How do I get the Seytrons app?

Download the Seytrons app from your official store link, open it, and tap Connect. Ads are blocked and privacy protection turns on automatically.
