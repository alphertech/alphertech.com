Stories — Our Journey & Team

From a two-person freelance shop to a full-service digital studio — our story is built on clients, craft, and community.

Founding

AlpherTech began in 2018 when two university classmates started building websites for local businesses in Kampala. Early projects focused on straightforward sites and small e-commerce stores. Word of mouth and a reputation for on-time delivery grew the business steadily.

Growth & Focus

By 2020 we expanded into mobile apps and cloud integrations. Our focus shifted to creating sustainable digital products with measurable outcomes — not just one-off websites. We partnered with local retailers, financial institutions, and NGOs to deliver scalable solutions adapted for East African contexts.

Community Work

We run a monthly workshop for students and small business owners on digital skills, and we provide pro-bono technical audits for select nonprofits each year. We believe building the local tech ecosystem helps everyone.
